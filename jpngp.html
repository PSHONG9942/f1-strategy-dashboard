Active Aero Modes

Suzuka demands a stable platform through the snail sections while maximizing terminal velocity on the defining straight. The 2026 active aero system makes these trade-offs explicit.

Corner Mode

Z-Mode deployed for max downforce. Cornering velocities drop 40-50 km/h to preserve energy.

Straight Mode

X-Mode cuts drag by 55% and downforce by 30%, maximizing straight acceleration.
