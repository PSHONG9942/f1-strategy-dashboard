Tyres & Strategy
Tribology & Cold Tearing

The track was entirely resurfaced in August 2024. A year later, the bitumen binder has worn away, exposing the aggregate stones. With track ambients at a chilling 15°C, failure to generate core bulk temperature in the C3 Mediums forces the rubber to slide over the stones rather than grip them. This physical tearing strips chunks of polymer from the tread—a catastrophic destruction of lap time.

Compound Constraint Analysis
  • ALLOCATION_PENALTY: Sprint weekend limits reduced to 12 sets total.
  • INVENTORY: 2x C2 (Hard) | 4x C3 (Medium) | 6x C4 (Soft).
  • DEGRADATION_MODEL: C4 Soft structural failure via deep-tread graining calculated within 5 laps of heavy fuel running.
  • STRAT_MANDATE: Universal C3 Medium start projected. High-load lateral wake (dirty air) in T7-T8 will violently accelerate sliding friction for following cars.
